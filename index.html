Institute for ResourceManagement & Economic Development

The Institute for Resource Management and Economic Development (IRMED) is an autonomous non-governmental, non-profit organization dedicated to the cause of public service mainly through education and research.Established and managed by a group of eminent multi-disciplinary professionals, the Institute has developed a high level of competence and has also acquired an international reputation for high quality output. Its research focus is on comprehensive and integrated approach to development and management of basic resources like land, water, forests, energy, manpower, which have profound impact on welfare of the public.It provides education to students for CIT and BCA degrees of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU).
