Infrastructure

The Institute has its own spacious building on a main road in an easily accessible institutional area of Delhi. The four storey building has enough space for office, research, class/seminar rooms, library and computer centre. The building is having all the necessary equipment and facilities: a standby generator, elevator, photocopier, projector, scanner, television, refrigerator, fax machine, air conditioners, water purification machine etc. Keeping in view the environmental concerns, the Institute has setup a solar system for supplementingthe energy needs for its own requirements on the roof top of its building.

Library

The Institute has a specialized library and reference centre containing valuable publications (books, reports and journals) in the area of resource studies, and socio-economic development. Any bona-fide researcher can use the library for in-house consultation during office hours. Issue of books and periodicals, however, is restricted only to its members as per the library rules.

Computer Centre

The Institute has a large Computer Centre with 30 computers and 2 printers complete with Internet access. The Centre has a generator backup, to work as stand by during power outages.

The Computer Centre provides facilities for:

  1. Research, training and consultancy assignments undertaken by the Institute.
  2. Regular correspondence and administrative work of the Institute.
  3. Education to students allotted to Institute by IGNOU for BCA degree courses.
  4. Processing and analysis of data for other institutions.

Institutions and individuals proposing to utilize the service of the Computer Centre should contact the Director of the Institute for availability, rates etc.
