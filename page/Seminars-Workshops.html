Training Programmes Organised

The Institute has played an active part in organizing seminars and conferences on important policy related issues. These have been organized in Delhi as well as in other parts of the country and have generally been sponsored by different ministries of Government of India, State Governments, international and other agencies. All the seminars and conferences have been well attended with the right mix of participation from government, academic institutions and NGOs depending upon the context. These seminars and conferences have come forward with very useful recommendations. The list of such seminars/conferences including the very famous International Conference on Sustainable Development of Water Resources held in November 2000 is given below.

  1. A workshop on "Critical Issues in Solid Waste Management in India and the Way Forward" was organised by the Institute in its Conference Hall at 2B, Institutional Area, Karkardooma, Delhi on 16th March, 2019.
  2. A workshop on “Odisha State Water Policy with Special Reference to Climate Change” sponsored by Global Water Partnership (GWP) through India Water Partnership (IWP) held at Bhubaneswar on 16th December, 2016.
  3. A workshop on “Karnataka State Water Policy with Special Reference to Climate Change” sponsored by Global Water Partnership (GWP) through India Water Partnership (IWP) held at Bangalore on 16th November, 2016.
  4. A workshop on “Goa State Water Policy with Special Reference to Climate Change” sponsored by Global Water Partnership (GWP) through India Water Partnership (IWP) held at Bardez, Goa on 2nd November, 2015.
  5. A workshop on “Tamil Nadu State Water Policy with Special Reference to Climate Change” sponsored by Global Water Partnership (GWP) through India Water Partnership (IWP) held at Chennai on 23rd July, 2015.
  6. A workshop on “Gujarat State Water Policy with Special Reference to Climate Change” sponsored by Global Water Partnership (GWP) through India Water Partnership (IWP) held at Gandhinagar (Gujarat) on 29thNovember, 2014.
  7. A workshop on “Approach to Bihar State Water Policy with Special Reference to Climate Change” sponsored by Global Water Partnership (GWP) through India Water Partnership (IWP) held at Patna (Bihar) on 14th June, 2014.
  8. A Workshop on "New Approach to Management of Water Resources in India" Sponsored by Department of Science & Technology, Government of India, held at New Delhi on 16thMarch 2007.
  9. Two days Workshop on "Water for Survival and Growth" for Secondary Level School Students of Government of NCT of Delhi during 24-25 February, 2007 sponsored by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.
  10. National Workshop on Community Approach to Flood Management in India sponsored by World Meteorological Organization (WMO), Geneva and National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA), Government of India, held at India International Centre in Delhi on November 28 and 29, 2005.
  11. Workshop on Strengthening of Flood Management Infrastructure in India sponsored by India Water Partnership, GWP-South Asia held at Central Water Commission, New Delhi on May 4, 2005.
  12. Dialogue Workshop on Dams and Development sponsored by India Water Partnership on behalf of GWP-SASTAC, held at Institute of Development Studies in Jaipur on March 2, 2005.
  13. Workshop on Municipal Solid Waste Management held at Institute premises in Delhi on September 17, 2003.
  14. Dialogue Workshop on Dams and Development sponsored by India Water Partnership on behalf of GWP-SASTAC, held at RCVP Naronha Academy of Administration and Management, Bhopal, August 10, 2002.
