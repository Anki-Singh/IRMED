Management

Organizational Setup

The Board of Management of the Institute oversees and guides its overall working. For day-to-day operations, the Institute functions under the leadership of the Chairman with the support of a Director and other faculties and research personnel at different levels. There is a separate administrative and accounts division to provide secretarial assistance. Besides, a number of eminent experts work as part time consultants as per needs.

Patron

Dr. M.S. Swaminathan, Renowned Agricultural Scientist, Former Dy. Chairman, Planning Commission & Former Secretary, Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India. President and award winner of several international bodies. Now chairman, M.S. Swaminathan Research Foundation, Chennai.
